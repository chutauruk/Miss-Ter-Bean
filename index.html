DIY Recipe

  • Prep Time: 2 Minutes
  • Cook Time: 2 Minutes
  • Equipment: High speed blender, (chilled) tall glass, spoon.
  • Ingredients: 2 Cups of crushed ice cubes, 1 cup of espresso/coffee, 3/4 cup of milk, 2 tbsp. sweetener, (optional) whipped cream
  • Instructions: Make the coffee. Blend all ingredients except for whipped cream. Pour drink into a chilled mug. Then, add the whip cream. Serve and enjoy your drink.
